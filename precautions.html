Precautions

Here are some precautions to keep in mind for car maintenance:

  • Regularly check tire pressure and tread depth.
  • Change the engine oil and filter as per the manufacturer's recommendations.
  • Inspect and replace worn-out brake pads and rotors.
  • Keep the car clean, both inside and outside, to prevent damage and corrosion.
  • Check and replace the air filter regularly for proper engine performance.
  • Ensure proper fluid levels for the engine coolant, brake fluid, and transmission fluid.
